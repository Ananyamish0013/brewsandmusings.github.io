ABOUT THIS BLOG

This blog is my very first attempt at creating a little corner of the internet for myself. I wanted it to feel warm, welcoming, and personal—like sitting down with a friend over a cup of coffee. The cozy coffee theme felt natural to me, since I’ve always loved the idea of conversations flowing easier with a warm drink in hand. It’s a space where I can share my thoughts, ideas, and little pieces of my journey.

When I started working on the design, I wasn’t sure how it would turn out. I played around with different looks until I found something that truly felt like “me.” The brown tones, coffee motifs, and soft style gave it the atmosphere I was hoping for. It feels like a calm café where stories, reflections, and creativity can take root.

A big part of this project was the sprite artwork. Making those little pixel pieces was both fun and surprisingly challenging. Each detail took time and patience, but seeing them come to life on the page made all the effort worth it. It gave the blog a unique touch, something handmade that reflects the care I put into it.

Looking back, I’d say the whole process was a learning experience I truly enjoyed. Even when it felt tough, I loved figuring things out and experimenting with new ideas. If I had the chance, I’d do it all over again in a heartbeat. This blog is just the start, and I’m excited to see where it grows from here.
