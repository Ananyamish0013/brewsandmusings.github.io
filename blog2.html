DAYS AT NSUT <3

My first days at NSUT have been nothing short of exciting. Walking through the gates for the first time, I felt a mix of nervousness and anticipation. Everything seemed so big and new, but slowly I began to settle in and soak up the energy of the campus. The initial jitters quickly gave way to curiosity as I started exploring different corners of the university.

One of the best parts of my experience so far has been the seniors. I was worried about how I would adjust, but they were incredibly welcoming and supportive. From guiding me around the campus to sharing little tips about classes and life here, their kindness made the transition much smoother. It really helped me feel at home in an environment that had felt intimidating at first.

Along the way, I’ve met so many new people from different backgrounds and with unique interests. It didn’t take long to bond over shared experiences and laughs, and I already feel like I’ve made friendships that will last throughout my time here. The sense of community is strong, and it’s exciting to think of all the memories we’ll create together in the next four years.

The classes have been engaging, and I’m enjoying learning in a more interactive and dynamic setting. Beyond academics, the campus itself is lively and full of surprises—whether it’s spotting a peacock crossing the path, watching cats laze around, or being greeted by the many friendly dogs. These little moments make the atmosphere vibrant and memorable.

Overall, my first days at NSUT have been a wonderful mix of learning, exploring, and connecting. With such a welcoming environment and so many exciting experiences ahead, I’m truly looking forward to making the most of my journey here.
